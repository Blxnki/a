What is hardcore-punk?

Hardcore punk is a subgenre of punk.

Characteristics

A more aggressive style in terms of instruments and vocals. The drums and guitars have faster rhythms, and the vocals are much more aggressive.

Chronology and Impact

The pioneering bands of the genre can be found in the United States in the late '70s and early '80s. Bands like Bad Brains, Black Flag, Minor Threat, Teen Idles, or DOA began playing influenced by the punk movement but with much faster music, shorter and more direct songs, and much more aggressive vocals than the pioneering punk bands like Sex Pistols or The Ramones.
