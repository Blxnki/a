Did you know...

Nu metal combines elements of metal with other genres like hip-hop, grunge, funk, and industrial. Bands like Limp Bizkit and Korn are known for incorporating these mixed styles.

Did you know...

Some nu metal bands include unusual instruments for the genre, like turntables and samplers, adding unique textures to their music.

Did you know...

Black metal originated in Norway in the late 80s and early 90s. Bands like Mayhem and Burzum are pioneers of the genre, known for its dark atmosphere and controversial aesthetic.

Did you know...

Many black metal bands are inspired by Norse mythology, gothic literature, and occultism. Lyrics often explore themes of misanthropy, darkness, and nature.

Did you know...

Death metal is known for its distinctive vocal style called "death growl," a guttural growl that adds a unique intensity to the music. Bands like Death and Cannibal Corpse are iconic in this aspect.

Did you know...

The lyrics of death metal often explore violent, macabre, and morbid themes, sometimes graphically. This has led to debates over censorship and freedom of expression in music.

Did you know...

Post-hardcore is characterized by its combination of aggressiveness and emotionality. The lyrics tend to be introspective and personal, while the music maintains high energy and technical complexity.
